About

Hello! I'm John, your private math and physics tutor. I study Applied and Pure Mathematics at the University of Waterloo, and graduated from Lakehead University with a diploma in Civil Engineering. My academic interests include analysis and differential geometry. I have been tutoring highschool, college, and university students online and in person for over 5 years. For fun I like to rock climb, mountain bike, play hockey, snowboard, cross country ski, and have just recently taken up golfing.
